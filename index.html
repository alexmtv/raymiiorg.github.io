About

I'm a Linux/Unix sysadmin with experience in High Availability, scaling and clustering, security, (Open)SSL and general linux system administration. I've worked as a sysadmin (devops) for Certificate Authorities, Hospitals, Managed Service providers, Datacenters Development shops and large Internet Service providers. This guide shows you how to use Openstack Affinity groups. Affinity or Anti-Affinity groups allow you to make sure instances (VM/VPS) are on the same hypervisor host or on a different one. There are cases when you want two instances on different compute nodes, for example, when they are clustered servers like a load balancer or a database master-master setup.
