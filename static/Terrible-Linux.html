Terrible Linux

Author: Remy Van Elst
Date: 01-01-2006



I don't like the changing Linux landscape. I, however love the "old" desktop way of working. You know, with a menu, taskbar, window buttons and a keyboard launcher. I also don't like a linux distribution that eats up resources. So, except for crunchbang and arch (and gentoo) there are at the moment no workable out of the box distro's for me which I know and like.
I used to like suse, mint and mandrake. Mandrake is no more,Ssuse is a resource hog, and mint is going the right way with their own desktop.
Now I know that it is all configurable, but when you have work to do you just want things to work. Tinkering is something for a free weekend, and that is when I customize my arch workstations, sun sparcstation, dos 5.5 monochrome 12 lbs laptop and other exotic hardware.(OS/2, BEOS, DOS, HAIKU, REACTOS)
So, thats why I made a custom debian respin with remastersys, and the software I use.

It is targeted at likeminded people, it requires some linux knowledge and it is very fast on my netbook, main workstation and my girlfriend is happy with it on her laptop.
