Hello! I'm a linux/unix sysadmin. I like the devops mindset and absolutely love configuration management frameworks like Ansible, Chef or Puppet. I've got lots of experience with a variety of free and open source software as well as with closed source business applications.

On this website you can find scripts and tutorials, mostly for GNU/Linux and other open source software. Also some homebrewed pieces of software you might find interesting. This is also my own public documentation archive.
